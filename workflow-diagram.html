ℹ️ i2v vs t2v: In i2v mode, Node 20 (LTXVImgToVideoAdvanced) replaces Node 6 (LTXVConditioning) + Node 7 (EmptyLTXVLatentVideo). It takes the uploaded image, encodes it through the VAE, and produces the initial latent + conditioning. All other nodes (1–5, 8–10, 15–18) are identical.
